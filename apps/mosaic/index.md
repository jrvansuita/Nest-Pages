## Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

### Types of Data Collected

#### Board Content You Create

When configuring a board, You may provide:

- **A board photo** — optionally, a photo of the person who will use the board.
- **Board profile metadata** — a name, an age range, and a gender, used to tailor the board's
  suggested vocabulary.
- **Tiles** — the words, labels, images, and icons that make up the board.

All of this content is **stored locally on Your device**. We do not host or store Your boards,
photos, or tiles on our own servers.

#### Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. It may include:

- Device type and OS version
- App version
- Anonymous usage patterns
- Crash logs (if enabled)

We use **Firebase Analytics** to track anonymous usage and app interactions.  
We use **Firebase Crashlytics** to collect crash reports that help us improve app stability.

## AI Image Generation

Mosaic offers an optional feature that generates a personalized tile image using artificial
intelligence so a tile can resemble the person who uses the board.

- **This feature is opt-in.** It is only available after a caregiver explicitly grants AI consent
  for a board. You can withdraw consent at any time (see *Revoking AI Consent* below).
- **What is sent.** When You generate an AI image, the board photo and the tile's text are sent to
  Google's Gemini API to produce the image. The generated image is returned to Your device and
  stored locally. We do not retain the photo or the generated image on our own servers.
- **Which API is used.** Mosaic uses the **Gemini Developer API** through **Firebase AI Logic**.
  Under the Gemini Developer API terms, Google may use the content You submit (prompts, images, and
  responses) to provide and improve its services, including to train its models. Please review
  Google's terms before using this feature:
  - **Gemini API Additional Terms of Service** – [See Terms](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms)
  - **Google Privacy Policy** – [See Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- If we ever change the underlying service (for example, to a tier that does not use submitted data
  for training), this page will be updated to reflect that.

### Revoking AI Consent

You can disable AI image generation at any time by turning off the **AI consent** toggle in the
board settings. Once disabled, no further content is sent to the Gemini API for that board.

## Retention of Your Data

- We do not store Your boards, photos, profile metadata, or generated images on our servers.
- All board content is stored locally on Your device.
- Content submitted to the Gemini API is handled by Google according to its own terms (see *AI Image

## Transfer of Your Data

content You explicitly submit for AI image generation (sent to Google's Gemini API), anonymous usage
and crash data, and anything You choose to share yourself (e.g. exporting a board backup file).
